Kiki Swing

During the release of "Tu es Foutu 2K12" Ingrid meets the band Réseau who have made the "Original Jazz Mix" e " Chill House Mix" versions of the super famous track.

Réseau is a duo made by Fabrizio Fratepietro and Sara Pittavini whose sound represents a unique blend of electro and jazz. Fabrizio is one of the top Italian drummer, founder of BabyraSoul produced by Jovanotti. His track-record credits co-operations with Gabin, Chris Cornell (Sound Garden), Moses (Zero Seven), Mia Cooper, Paola Turci, Daniele Silvestri, Alex Britti, Riccardo Sinigallia, Chiara Civello, Nicola Piovani.

Sara is an architect and eclectic artist. She melts unexpected Tap performances with the magic charm of her virtual art. The outcome is a surprising AudioVisual suggestive performance. Réseau's sophisticated sound is perfectly featured in their first album "Abbey Song".

Recently Ingrid's co-operation with Rèseau has deeply developed and implemented and has finally lead to her new single "Kiki Swing/Kiki La Belle".

The song tells of a magic feminine image, a sort of "silhouette d'amour" whose melodious moves fascinate the whole word.
